Your Hosts

Meet the Voices

Three friends, three perspectives — one very honest conversation.

Dr. Elizabeth Johnson
Licensed Psychologist · CA & NY

Dr. Liz is a licensed psychologist in private practice, offering therapy, assessment, and consultation. A Stanford-trained clinician with deep expertise in identity, equity, and mental health, she's the voice on the show who makes the research make sense — without losing the human in it.

Emily Connery
HR Professional in Tech

Emily has spent 15 years working in HR in the tech industry — which means she has opinions about workplace boundaries, burnout, and why "unlimited PTO" is often a trap. She brings the working-parent perspective: the mental load, the calendar chaos, and the constant negotiation between who you are at work and who you want to be at home.

Emily Llobell
Consultant · ABA Specialist

Emily is a consultant specializing in autism, Applied Behavior Analysis, and individualized learning. With a master's in Psychological Counseling and advanced ABA certification, she's the person on the show who helps you actually understand your kid — and yourself.
